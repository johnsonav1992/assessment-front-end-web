Bursey Ranch

Bursey Ranch is a mid-sized housing community in Keller with the first builds started in 1985. It is located at the corner of Willis Lane and North Tarrant Parkway in Keller, TX. Bursey Ranch is conveniently located directly East of Indian Springs Middle School and just south of Willis Lane Elementary. Some notable nearby restaurants within walking distance are Simply Thai, WhoDaq, IHOP, Lupe's Tex Mex and Grill, Waffle House, and Niki's Italian Bistro.
